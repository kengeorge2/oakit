Most POS systems were designed for Western markets and bolted on African payment methods as an afterthought. ClassicPOS was built from the ground up for East African businesses. Here's what that means in practice:

Accept Every Payment Method

Your customers pay in different ways. ClassicPOS handles them all through a single terminal:

  • Cash — Traditional cash payments with automatic change calculation
  • MTN Mobile Money — The most popular payment method in Uganda, natively integrated
  • Airtel Money — Full support for Airtel Money transactions

Internet outages are a reality in East Africa. ClassicPOS keeps your business running even when connectivity drops. All transactions are stored locally and automatically synced when the connection returns. Your sales never stop.
